THE TEN COMMANDMENTS

1. I am the Lord your God; you shall not have strange gods before me.
  • Have I doubted or denied God's existence?
  • Have I failed to pray daily?
  • Have I put other things before God?
2. You shall not take the name of the Lord your God in vain.
  • Have I used God's name carelessly or irreverently?
  • Have I sworn falsely or made false promises?
3. Remember to keep holy the Lord's day.
  • Have I missed Mass on Sunday or Holy Days?
  • Have I been inattentive at Mass?
  • Have I done unnecessary work on Sunday?
4. Honor your father and your mother.
  • Have I disobeyed my parents?
  • Have I failed to help them when needed?
  • Have I been disrespectful to authority?
5. You shall not kill.
  • Have I harmed others physically or emotionally?
  • Have I been angry or held grudges?
  • Have I supported abortion or euthanasia?
6. You shall not commit adultery.
  • Have I been unfaithful to my spouse?
  • Have I engaged in impure thoughts or actions?
  • Have I used others for my own pleasure?
7. You shall not steal.
  • Have I taken what doesn't belong to me?
  • Have I cheated or been dishonest?
  • Have I failed to pay debts?
8. You shall not bear false witness against your neighbor.
  • Have I lied or spread false rumors?
  • Have I gossiped or slandered others?
  • Have I failed to defend others' reputations?
9. You shall not covet your neighbor's wife.
  • Have I had impure thoughts about others?
  • Have I been envious of others' relationships?
10. You shall not covet your neighbor's goods.
  • Have I been envious of others' possessions?
  • Have I been greedy or materialistic?
  • Have I failed to be generous to those in need?
